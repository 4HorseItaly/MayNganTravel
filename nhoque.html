NHO QUE RIVER: THE EMERALD GEM

/ Guest

  • Duration: 2 Days 1 Night
  • Departure: Ha Giang
  • Transportation: Private Car/Bus
Book This Tour

Trip Overview

  • Popular Destinations: Nho Que River, Ma Pi Leng Pass, Dong Van Karst Plateau Geopark, H'Mong Village.
  • Dining Experience: Enjoy local dishes and authentic Ha Giang cuisine.
  • Recommended For: Adventure Seekers, Nature Lovers, Photographers.
  • Best Travel Period: Year-round, with different beauties in each season.
  • Special Offers: Check website for seasonal discounts.

Itinerary

Day 1: HA GIANG CITY – DONG VAN KARST PLATEAU

Morning: The Journey Begins

  • The tour guide will pick you up from Ha Giang city and start the trip to Dong Van.
  • Enjoy the scenic route, stopping at Quan Ba ​​Heaven Gate to admire the Twin Mountains.

Noon: Dong Van Ancient Town

  • Have lunch in Dong Van and explore the ancient town with traditional architecture and local markets.

Afternoon: Conquering Ma Pi Leng Pass

  • Drive through Ma Pi Leng Pass, one of the four highest passes in Vietnam. Stop to take photos and enjoy the panoramic view of Nho Que River.

Evening: Homestay in a H'Mong Village

  • Check-in at a local homestay in a H'Mong village near Dong Van.
  • Enjoy dinner with local dishes and experience the culture of the H'Mong people.

Day 2: NHO QUE RIVER – MENG VILLAGE - HA GIANG

Morning: Cruise on Nho Que River

  • Take a boat trip on the Nho Que River. Enjoy the breathtaking view of the river winding through the mountains.

Noon: Lunch with a View

  • Have lunch at a restaurant with a view of the river.

Afternoon: Explore H'Mong Village

  • Visit a H'Mong village to learn about their daily life and traditional crafts.
  • Head back to Ha Giang city in the late afternoon.

Evening: End of Journey

  • Arrive in Ha Giang City. Tour ends. May Ngan Travel says goodbye and looks forward to meeting you again!
