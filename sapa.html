Itinerary

Day 1: STONE CHURCH – SAPA LAKE – CAT CAT VILLAGE

Morning: Departure from the Land of Tea

  • 04:00: May Ngan Travel’s vehicle picks you up at the meeting point (Thai Nguyen) to begin our journey to the Northwest Highlands. Relax on board and catch the first rays of dawn along the majestic mountain passes.
  • 09:00: Arrive in Sapa town. Take a moment to breathe in the signature crisp, cool highland air.

Noon: Center Check-in & Local Flavors

  • 10:00: Visit and take photos at the Sapa Stone Church – the most intact ancient Gothic architectural icon left by the French. Enjoy a peaceful stroll around the romantic Sapa Lake.
  • 11:30: Lunch at O Quy Ho Restaurant, featuring bold Northwest flavors.
  • 13:00: Check-in at Suong May Sapa Homestay – where you can watch the clouds drift by right from your window.

Afternoon: Cultural Heritage Discovery

  • 14:30: Head to Cat Cat Village. Explore at your leisure, take photos by the waterfalls and giant water wheels, and learn about the traditional weaving culture of the H’Mong people.

Evening: Mountain Town Colors

  • 18:30: Dinner at Dung Thu Restaurant.
  • 20:00: Free time to explore Sapa Night Market, enjoy street-side grilled delicacies, and soak in the vibrant nightlife of the town.

Day 2: FANSIPAN – MUONG HOA VALLEY – SWING SAPA

Morning: Touching the Clouds

  • 07:00: Breakfast and check-out.
  • 08:00: Transfer to the cable car station to conquer Fansipan. Experience the sensation of gliding over a sea of clouds and check-in at the 3,143m summit – the "Roof of Indochina."

Noon: The Dreamy Valley

  • 11:00: Descend and travel to Muong Hoa Valley, home to ancient carved stones and stunning terraced rice fields.
  • 12:00: Lunch and rest at Mam Restaurant & Coffee. Enjoy the tranquil atmosphere in the heart of the valley.

Afternoon: "Little Bali" Vibes

  • 13:00: Free time for photos and sightseeing in Muong Hoa.
  • 14:30: Visit Swing Sapa – a trendy check-in spot featuring giant sculptures and a panoramic view of Hoang Lien Son National Park.
  • 16:00: Board the vehicle for the return trip to Thai Nguyen. End of an emotional journey with May Ngan Travel.
