MU CANG CHAI: GOLDEN RICE FIELDS

/ Guest

  • Duration: 3 Days 2 Nights
  • Departure: Hanoi
  • Transportation: Sleeper Bus/Private Car
Book This Tour

Trip Overview

  • Popular Destinations: La Pan Tan Rice Terraces, Che Cu Nha, Ba Nha Bridge, Lim Mong Village.
  • Dining Experience: Taste local dishes such as sticky rice, grilled meat, and other authentic specialties.
  • Recommended For: Photographers, Nature Lovers, Cultural Explorers.
  • Best Travel Period: September - October (for golden rice season).
  • Special Offers: Early bird discounts available, check the website for details.

Itinerary

Day 1: HANOI - MU CANG CHAI

Morning: Departure from Hanoi

  • Pick up from your hotel in Hanoi and depart for Mu Cang Chai.
  • Enjoy the scenic drive through the mountains and valleys of Northern Vietnam.

Noon: Lunch in Nghia Lo Town

  • Have lunch in Nghia Lo town, known for its delicious local cuisine.

Afternoon: Arrival in Mu Cang Chai - Check in

  • Arrive in Mu Cang Chai in the afternoon.
  • Check-in at your hotel/homestay and relax.

Evening: Dinner and Rest

  • Enjoy dinner with local specialties and rest for the night.

Day 2: EXPLORING MU CANG CHAI RICE TERRACES

Morning: Sunrise over the Rice Fields

  • Wake up early to enjoy the sunrise over the stunning rice terraces.
  • Visit La Pan Tan, Che Cu Nha, known as the most beautiful terraces in Mu Cang Chai.

Noon: Lunch with a View

  • Have lunch at a local restaurant with a view of the rice terraces.

Afternoon: Visit Local Villages and Ba Nha Bridge

  • Visit Lim Mong village to experience local culture.
  • Explore Ba Nha Bridge, one of the oldest bridge here.

Day 3: MU CANG CHAI - HANOI

Morning: Visit more Rice Terraces

  • Enjoy a last view of Mu Cang Chai before going back to Hanoi.

Afternoon: Transfer back to Hanoi
