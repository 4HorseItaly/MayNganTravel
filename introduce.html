OPENING REMARKS

"Travel is not merely a trip; it is a journey of discovery, sensing, and preserving the beauty and essence of cultural identity."

With that spirit, May Ngan Travel is on a mission to be your professional companion in the Northwest of Vietnam. We aim to help you "touch" the wild, majestic beauty of the great mountains in the right way and bring these "hidden gems in the clouds" to the global stage.

With May Ngan Travel, your journey through rugged paths, remote peaks, and sacred forests becomes safe and unforgettable. Every emotion and moment of connection with the nature, culture, and people of the Northwest will be captured‚Äînot just in photographs, but in the hearts of every traveler.

CORE VALUES

At May Ngan Travel, our core values serve as the North Star for every journey:

  • Authenticity: We prioritize genuine experiences and true cultural values, allowing travelers to touch the "soul of the mountains" in its most honest form.
  • Responsibility: We promote green tourism and environmental protection, standing side-by-side with local communities to preserve and promote precious cultural traditions.
  • Dedication: Above all, dedication is our bridge. Every member of May Ngan strives to understand and care for our guests with the sincerity one would show their own family.

  • Cultural & Culinary Experiences: Immersing you in local flavors and traditions.
