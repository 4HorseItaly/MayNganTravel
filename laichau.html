Trip Overview

  • Popular Destinations: O Quy Ho Pass, Heaven’s Gate, Pu Sam Cap Cave, Sin Suoi Ho Village, Heart Waterfall.
  • Dining Experience: Breakfast buffet, curated set menus, and authentic local specialties.
  • Recommended For: Couples, Families, and Youth.
  • Best Travel Period: Year-round.
  • Special Offers: Discounts are already included in the tour price.

Itinerary

Day 1: O QUY HO PASS – HEAVEN’S GATE

Morning: The Journey Begins

  • The vehicle and tour guide pick you up for departure. Along the way, stop to rest and enjoy unique roadside local snacks.
  • 09:00: Conquer O Quy Ho Pass – the longest and most majestic pass in the Northwest. At an altitude of over 2,000m, visit Heaven’s Gate, where clouds linger year-round. This is the perfect spot for photography and "cloud hunting" amidst the vast wilderness.

Noon: Mountain Flavors

  • Enjoy lunch at O Quy Ho Restaurant. Afterward, enjoy free time to explore, take photos, and experience activities at the O Quy Ho Tourist Area.

Afternoon: Welcome to Lai Chau City

  • Depart for Lai Chau City.
  • Check-in and relax at Muong Thanh Hotel – a luxury accommodation providing comfort and elegance.

Evening: Nightlife in the Mountain City

  • Savor a cozy dinner at the hotel restaurant.
  • After dinner, explore Lai Chau City at night, soaking in the peaceful beauty of this borderland before returning to the hotel for rest.

Day 2: PU SAM CAP CAVE – SIN SUOI HO VILLAGE – HEART WATERFALL

Morning: Highland Specialties & Cave Wonders

  • Start your day with a special breakfast: the famous Bac Ha Black Chicken Pho, renowned for its rich and savory broth.
  • Head to Pu Sam Cap Cave – a national scenic site dubbed the "First Cave of the Northwest." Be mesmerized by the magnificent natural stalactite systems at altitudes ranging from 1,300m to 1,700m.

Noon: Hospitable Sin Suoi Ho Village

  • Arrive at Sin Suoi Ho, a model community-based tourism village. Feel free to take photos, interact with locals, and learn about their rustic lifestyle.
  • Lunch and rest at Villa De La Roseraie Homestay – a tranquil space in the heart of the village.

Afternoon: Heart Waterfall & Legendary Fair Market

  • Visit the H'Mong village and Heart Waterfall, a pristine natural wonder associated with a legend of eternal, faithful love.
  • Shop for handmade crafts and local produce at the local fair market before boarding the vehicle for the return trip to Thai Nguyen.

Evening: End of Journey

  • Arrive back in Thai Nguyen. May Ngan Travel bids you farewell and looks forward to seeing you on your next adventure!
