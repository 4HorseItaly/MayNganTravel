  • Dining Experience: Breakfast buffet, curated set menus, and authentic local specialties.
  • Recommended For: Couples, Families, and Youth.
  • Best Travel Period: Year-round.
  • Special Offers: Discounts are already included in the tour price.

Itinerary

Day 1: WHITE STONE PASS – MAI CHAU VALLEY – HEART TEA HILL

Morning: Heading to the Highland Steppe

  • May Ngan Travel’s vehicle and guide pick you up to begin the journey to the rustic land of Son La.
  • Stop at White Stone Pass (Thung Khe Pass) to rest and admire the breathtaking scenery. From here, you can enjoy a panoramic view of the beautiful Mai Chau Valley veiled in mysterious morning mist.

Noon: Highland Flavors

  • Arrive in Moc Chau and enjoy lunch at a local restaurant featuring famous specialties from the Northwest mountains.

Afternoon: The "Kingdom" of Tea and Plums

  • Visit the Heart-Shaped Tea Hill: You will be enchanted by the endless lush green tea rows artistically shaped into hearts. This is the perfect spot to breathe in the fresh air and capture stunning photographs.

Evening: Plateau Festival Night

  • Check-in at Hoa Sua Homestay, a cozy and welcoming space.
  • After dinner, you can participate in a Campfire, enjoy traditional music performances, join the "Sap" dance, and sip "Ruou Can" (straw wine) with the locals (upon request)—an unmissable cultural connection.

Day 2: HAPPY LAND – BAN ANG PINE FOREST

Morning: Conquering World Records

  • Enjoy breakfast and complete the check-out process.
  • Visit Happy Land: Known as the "Most Beautiful Flower Garden in Moc Chau," featuring vibrant floral carpets year-round and various interactive spaces.
  • Conquer the Bach Long Glass Bridge: A world-record-breaking architectural feat with a length of 632m. Experience the thrill of "walking on air" while gazing at the deep abyss and majestic forests from a breathtaking height.

Noon: Rest & Relaxation at Ban Ang

  • Move to the pine forest area for lunch and relaxation in a cool, pleasant atmosphere.

Afternoon: "Little Da Lat" in the North

  • Visit Ban Ang Pine Forest: With green pine canopies reflected in the still lake, this place looks like a romantic oil painting. You can take a leisurely stroll or bike around the lake to feel its pristine, poetic beauty.
  • Bid farewell to Moc Chau and begin the journey back. Stop at Moc Chau Farm to shop for local specialties (fresh milk, milk cakes, tea, etc.) as gifts for loved ones.

Evening: End of Journey

  • Arrive back at the original meeting point, concluding your 2D1N Moc Chau adventure. May Ngan Travel thanks you and hopes to see you on your next adventure!
